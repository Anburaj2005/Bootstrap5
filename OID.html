Who Uses Form 1099-OID?

Based on the cases below, A 1099-OID Form has been issued

  • When users pay at least $10 of reportable OID to an individual
  • When the user has withheld and paid any foreign tax on OID for someone
  • When the user withheld and didn't refund federal income tax from someone under the backup withholding rules.

Generally

  • Form 1099-OID is sent by a brokerage holding the debt instruments for investors with OID.
  • As an investor, the user does not want to issue a tax return form unless the user pays the OID to someone else.
  • If the user has an investment with an OID, they should receive their form in the mail at the beginning of each year in order to complete the information asked on the tax return.
