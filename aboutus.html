COVID-19 Care at the office

Here at Tax2efile we are making sure our workplaces are clean and hygienic by:

  • Surfaces (e.g. desks and tables) and objects (e.g. telephones, keyboards) being wiped with disinfectant regularly.
  • Promoting regular and thorough hand-washing by employees & employers.
  • Putting hand sanitizer rub dispensers in prominent places around the workplace.
  • Making sure these dispensers are refilled regularly.
  • Promoting good respiratory hygiene in the workplace.
  • Ensuring that face masks(surgical mask) and / or paper tissues are available, for those who develop a runny nose or cough at work, along with closed bins for hygienically disposing of them.
  • Refraining from unnecessary travel both local and international.
