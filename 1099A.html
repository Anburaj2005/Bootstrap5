Who Uses Form 1099-A?

  • If there is more than one owner of undivided interest in a single loan, the trustee or registration owner must file a 1099-A form on behalf of all owners.
  • If a loan is transferred from one holder to another, the subsequent loan holder must report the events of the transfer on Form 1099-A.
  • If a government unit or subsidiary agent loans money that is secured by property, the government unit must submit form 1099-A.
  • If there are several lenders of money secured by the property, and a lender seizes on a building, the other trust holders must file Form 1099-A.
